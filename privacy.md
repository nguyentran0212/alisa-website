      <h1 class="section-title" style="text-align:left; margin-bottom:40px;">Privacy Policy</h1>

      <p>Last updated: April 2026</p>

      <p>Alisa Play Therapy ("I", "me", or "my") operates the website alisaplaytherapy.com and is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children and families I work with. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard information provided through this website.</p>

      <h2>Information I Collect</h2>
      <p>If you choose to contact me through this website, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message. I collect this information solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and, if appropriate, arranging play therapy services.</p>

      <p>I do not collect sensitive health information through this website. If you choose to share details about your child's needs or circumstances in your message, that information is provided voluntarily by you.</p>

      <h2>How I Use Your Information</h2>
      <p>Information submitted through the contact form is used to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Respond to your enquiry</li>
        <li>Discuss whether play therapy may be appropriate for your child</li>
        <li>Arrange initial consultations or sessions</li>
        <li>Provide relevant information about my services</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Form Submissions</h2>
      <p>Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree, Inc., a third-party email service. Your submission data (name, email, and message content) is sent directly to Formspree and used solely to deliver your message to me. Formspree's use of your data is governed by their own <a href="https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Privacy Policy</a>.</p>

      <h2>Cookies and Analytics</h2>
      <p>This website does not use cookies and does not employ analytics tracking software.</p>

      <h2>Data Retention</h2>
      <p>Emails and messages received through this website are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or to manage ongoing therapeutic work. If you become a client, your information will be managed in accordance with my professional obligations as a registered play therapist.</p>

      <h2>Data Security</h2>
      <p>I take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide. However, please note that electronic communication inherently carries some risk, and I cannot guarantee the complete security of data transmitted over the internet.</p>

      <h2>Disclosure of Information</h2>
      <p>I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information will not be shared with any other therapist, organisation, or third party without your explicit consent, except where required by law.</p>

      <h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
      <p>This website is intended for parents and caregivers. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 through this website. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact me immediately.</p>

      <h2>Your Rights</h2>
      <p>You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided through this website. To exercise these rights or if you have any concerns about how your information is being handled, please contact me using the details on the <a href="/#contact">Contact page</a>.</p>

      <h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
      <p>I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.</p>

      <h2>Contact</h2>
      <p>If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is being handled, please reach out via the <a href="/#contact">Contact page</a>.</p>
